How this practice helps

This activity helps you train speech clarity. You hear a phrase, say it aloud, and get immediate feedback. Repeating successful attempts helps lock in mouth movement patterns, and later review checks help you retain what you learned.

Your task

  1. Click Play Phrase to hear the model phrase.
  2. Click Record to start. Recording stops automatically once speech is captured.
  3. Read the feedback and try again as needed.
  4. Track your overall improvement in the progress panel.

Tip: Speak naturally and clearly, not too fast.
